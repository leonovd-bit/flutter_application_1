### Font Choices
- **Headlines**: Poppins (Bold, friendly, food-focused)

### Text Hierarchy
- **Display Large**: 32px, Weight 900 (Brand names, hero text)

- **Headlines**: 18-22px, Weight 600-700 (Section headers)
- **Body**: 14-16px, Weight 400-500 (Content text)
